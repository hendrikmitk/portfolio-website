About

My name is Hendrik Harlichs, I am years old and live in Hamburg. My first and for a long time only encounter with software development was when I participated in an HTML workgroup at school in 8th grade. That was in 2002.

Since then, I have learned how to fix cars, got my technical college degree, taught CMS training in Sweden and South Korea, and eventually became a project manager in software development which, on a side note, landed me aboard empty cruise ships.

Solving concrete problems and immersing myself in technical contexts has always given me much more joy than the usual activities of a project manager. In 2020, I decided to turn passion into profession.

I soon will complete the full-stack web development program at Hamburg Coding School and am currently looking for my first position as a frontend or full-stack developer in Hamburg or remote.

You have or know about such a position? Drop me a line.
