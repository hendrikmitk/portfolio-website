About

My name is Hendrik Harlichs, I am years old and live in Hamburg. My first and long time only encounter with software development was participating in an HTML workgroup in 8th grade. That was in 2002.

Since then, I learned how to fix cars, taught CMS training and eventually became a project manager in software development, which landed me aboard empty cruise ships.

Solving concrete problems and immersing myself in technical contexts have always given me the greatest pleasure. In 2020, I decided to turn passion into profession.

I graduated from the Full-Stack Web Development program at Hamburg Coding School in March 2021. After that I joined appmotion as Junior Frontend Developer and since 05/2022 I am with CHECK24 in Hamburg.
